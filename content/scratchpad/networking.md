## Cellular
 - 1G analog 30 kHz FM Modulation, FDMA
 - 2G digital, Glabal System or Mobile Communications (GSM) 9.6bkps circuit switched, 200 kHz, FDMA+TDMA (8 slots), GMSK modulation
   - General Packet Radio Service (GPRS 2.5G) packet switching 171 kbps
   - (EDGE/EGPRS 2.75G) 384 kbps, 8PSK modulation
 - 3G Universal Mobile Telecommunications System (UMTS), 5MHz, CDMA (spread spectrum), QPSK madulation, 960 kbps
   - High Speed Packet Access (HSPA+), CDMA + DTMA, 337,5Mbps down 23Mbps up
 - 4G Long Term Evolution (LTE), OFDM, IP packets, 1Gbps
 - 5G massive increase of data rate, decrease of latency envisioned, Internet-of-Things, new radio
technologies, “softwarization”, virtualization, QoS signaling/network slicing
 - 6G area of research (2030)
